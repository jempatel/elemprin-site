Case Study

Autonomous Growth Engine

CDGO + Critique dual-role engine running 3-hour cycles to ship UX, content, and ops artifacts without founder bottlenecks.

The Challenge

Founder review cycles slowed UX/SEO/tech progress. Needed autonomous execution with human auditing.

Architecture

  • Role A: Chief Digital Growth Officer (CDGO) — aggressive optimization and delivery.
  • Role B: Strategic Critique — risk, brand, and technical debt checks.
  • Cadence: 3-hour cycles logging outputs to Drive for auditability.

Proof of Work

  • Homepage wireframe v1 with agentic status bar, SD-WAN emphasis, and tech stack section.
  • Case study drafts and LinkedIn authority post (internal artifacts).
  • Industry trends log feeding insights section.

Extensibility

  • Autonomous PR generation for tech fixes.
  • Dynamic A/B testing for UX.
  • Reputational loops: publish case studies based on milestone completions.
